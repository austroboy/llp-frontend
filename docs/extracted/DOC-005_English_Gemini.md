The following Act passed by the Parliament received the assent of the President on 14 November, 2018 corresponding to 30 Kartik, 1425 and is hereby published for general information:—

Act No. 58 of 2018
An Act enacted for further amendment of the Bangladesh Labour Act, 2006

Whereas it is expedient and necessary to further amend the Bangladesh Labour Act, 2006 (Act No. 42 of 2006) for fulfilling the following objectives;

Therefore, the following Act is hereby made:—

1. Short title and commencement.—(1) This Act shall be called the Bangladesh Labour (Amendment) Act, 2018.

(2) This Act shall come into force immediately.

2. Amendment of Section 1 of Act No. 42 of 2006.—Clause (e) of sub-section (4) of Section 1 of the Bangladesh Labour Act, 2006 (Act No. 42 of 2006), hereinafter referred to as the said Act, shall be omitted.

10. Substitution of Section 93 of Act No. 42 of 2006.—For Section 93 of the said Act, the following Section 93 shall be substituted, namely:—

“93. Dining rooms, etc.—(1) In every establishment where usually more than 25 (twenty-five) workers are employed, the necessary number of suitable dining rooms with arrangements for drinking water shall be provided and maintained so that the workers can eat the food brought by them and rest:

Provided that, any canteen maintained under Section 92 shall be deemed to be part of the necessary arrangements under this sub-section:

(2) Dining rooms shall be adequately lit and maintained clean, with ventilation facilities and at a tolerable temperature.”
